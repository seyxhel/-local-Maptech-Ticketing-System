# Kare-Kare

## Ingredients
- 1 kg oxtail and/or beef tripe, cleaned
- 8 cups water
- 1 onion, chopped
- 3 tbsp annatto oil (atsuete oil)
- 1/2 cup peanut butter
- 1/4 cup ground toasted rice (or rice flour)
- 1 bunch pechay
- 1 bundle sitaw, cut into 2-inch pieces
- 1 eggplant, sliced
- Banana heart (optional), sliced
- Fish sauce or salt, to taste
- Ground pepper, to taste

## For Bagoong Dip
- Sauteed shrimp paste

## Instructions
1. Boil oxtail/tripe with onion until very tender, around 1.5 to 2 hours. Reserve broth.
2. In a pot, heat annatto oil and add tender meat.
3. Pour in about 4 cups broth and bring to a simmer.
4. Add peanut butter and stir well.
5. Add ground rice little by little to thicken.
6. Season with fish sauce or salt and pepper.
7. Add eggplant and sitaw; cook 4 to 5 minutes.
8. Add pechay and cook 1 minute.
9. Serve with bagoong on the side.

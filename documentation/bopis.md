# Bopis

## Ingredients
- 500 g pork lungs and heart, cleaned and boiled until tender
- 3 tbsp cooking oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 thumb ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1/2 cup water
- 2 to 3 red chili, chopped
- 1 carrot, finely diced (optional)
- 1 bell pepper, finely diced (optional)
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Boil lungs and heart until cooked. Drain and finely chop.
2. Heat oil and saute onion, garlic, and ginger.
3. Add chopped organs and stir-fry for 5 to 7 minutes.
4. Add soy sauce, vinegar, and water.
5. Simmer for 8 to 10 minutes until liquid reduces.
6. Add chili and optional vegetables.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Serve hot as ulam or pulutan.

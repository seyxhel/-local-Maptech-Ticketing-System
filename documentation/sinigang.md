# Sinigang na Baboy

## Ingredients
- 1 kg pork ribs or belly, cut into serving pieces
- 8 cups water
- 2 tomatoes, quartered
- 1 onion, quartered
- 1 radish (labanos), sliced
- 1 eggplant, sliced
- 1 bundle sitaw, cut into 2-inch pieces
- 1 to 2 cups kangkong
- 2 to 3 green chili (siling pang-sigang)
- 1 pack sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind if available)
- Fish sauce (patis), to taste
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. In a pot, boil water and add pork, onion, and tomatoes.
2. Simmer for 40 to 60 minutes until pork is tender. Skim scum as needed.
3. Add radish and simmer 5 minutes.
4. Add eggplant and sitaw, then simmer 4 to 5 minutes.
5. Add sinigang mix and stir until dissolved.
6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
7. Add kangkong and green chili. Cook 1 to 2 minutes.
8. Serve hot with rice.

# Chicken Adobo

## Ingredients
- 1 kg chicken (thighs/drumsticks, or mixed)
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup vinegar (cane or white)
- 1 cup water
- 1 head garlic, minced or crushed
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp whole black peppercorns (or 1/2 tsp ground pepper)
- 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional)
- 2 tbsp cooking oil
- 1 medium onion, sliced (optional)
- Salt to taste (only if needed)

## Instructions
1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce and half the garlic for 20 to 30 minutes (optional but recommended).
2. Heat oil in a pot. Saute remaining garlic and onion until fragrant.
3. Add chicken (reserve marinade) and sear 2 to 3 minutes per side.
4. Pour in reserved marinade, vinegar, water, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
5. Do not stir for the first 2 to 3 minutes after adding vinegar.
6. Lower heat, cover, and simmer for 30 to 40 minutes until chicken is tender.
7. Add sugar if using. Simmer uncovered for 8 to 12 minutes to reduce sauce.
8. Taste and adjust seasoning. Add a little water if too salty, or simmer longer if too thin.
9. Serve hot with steamed rice.

## Tips
- For pork adobo, use pork belly or shoulder and simmer 45 to 60 minutes.
- For richer flavor, pan-fry chicken pieces after simmering, then return to sauce.
- Adobo tastes even better the next day.

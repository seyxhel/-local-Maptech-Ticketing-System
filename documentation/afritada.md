# Chicken Afritada

## Ingredients
- 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
- 3 tbsp cooking oil
- 1 onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 potatoes, cubed
- 2 carrots, cubed
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 green bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup tomato sauce
- 1 cup water or broth
- 2 tbsp fish sauce or soy sauce
- 1 bay leaf
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 1 tsp sugar (optional)
- Green peas (optional)

## Instructions
1. Heat oil and saute onion and garlic.
2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
3. Add fish sauce, tomato sauce, water, and bay leaf.
4. Cover and simmer 20 minutes.
5. Add potatoes and carrots; simmer 10 to 12 minutes.
6. Add bell peppers and peas. Cook 3 to 4 minutes.
7. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar if needed.
8. Serve hot with rice.

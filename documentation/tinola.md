# Chicken Tinola

## Ingredients
- 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
- 8 cups water
- 1 onion, sliced
- 5 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 thumb ginger, sliced into strips
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 green papaya (or sayote), sliced
- 1 bundle chili leaves or malunggay
- 2 tbsp cooking oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat oil and saute garlic, onion, and ginger.
2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
3. Add fish sauce and stir for 1 minute.
4. Pour in water and bring to a boil.
5. Simmer for 25 to 30 minutes until chicken is tender.
6. Add papaya or sayote and cook 8 to 10 minutes.
7. Add chili leaves or malunggay and cook 1 minute.
8. Season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.
